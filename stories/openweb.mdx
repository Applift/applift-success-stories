## About

OpenWeb’s goal is to improve online conversation by helping publishers and brands grow direct audience relationships through comment sections and community features.

In 2021, OpenWeb partnered with Applift to lead and own its iOS and Android SDKs, powering seamless integrations across top publisher apps such as Fox News and The Times, while addressing diverse technical and extensive customization requirements.

## Three Platforms, One Standard
Applift’s engineers led the development of OpenWeb’s iOS and Android SDKs, working as an integrated part of their mobile team. As the team grew, we continued to drive the SDKs forward in close collaboration-establishing shared engineering standards, review practices, and an MVVM architecture across both codebases. A full CI release pipeline publishes builds to CocoaPods and SPM for iOS, and Maven for Android.

Driven by growing demand from OpenWeb’s React Native publishers, Applift designed and implemented OpenWeb’s React Native SDK from the ground up in just five months. Engineered in TypeScript and architected to wrap the existing native SDKs through Fabric and Turbo Modules, the SDK delivers native-grade performance and reliability while giving publishers a first-class, type-safe integration experience.
