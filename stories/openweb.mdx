OpenWeb's mission is to improve online conversation. They partner with publishers and brands to build strong, direct audience relationships through healthy conversations and social experiences. OpenWeb works with more than 1,000 top-tier publishers and needed cross-platform SDKs with customizable UIs, flexible integration architecture, and built-in monetization — all at massive scale.
</Challenge>

<Partnership>
OpenWeb needed a team that could move fast across three platforms simultaneously — iOS, Android, and React Native — while maintaining the quality bar expected by 1,000+ publishers. Applift's engineers embedded into OpenWeb's mobile group, contributing to all three SDKs in parallel with consistent standards and shared code review practices.

The partnership required balancing speed with flexibility. Publishers have wildly different tech stacks, brand requirements, and integration preferences. Applift worked closely with OpenWeb's product team to design an architecture flexible enough to handle this diversity without sacrificing developer experience.
</Partnership>
