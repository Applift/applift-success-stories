## About

RiseUp helps people improve their financial bottom line. Their method is specific: a smart digital platform backed by real human support. The team had built something people trusted. What they didn't have was a mobile app.

Not one. Not iOS, not Android. Customers were using the product through the browser, and it was showing.

They came to Applift looking for more than developers. They needed someone willing to own the foundation, make the right architectural calls early, and stay long enough to see it through. Applift put four engineers in. Those four built the infrastructure and set the standards that would carry RiseUp's mobile development forward. When the internal team eventually took over, they'd be inheriting something solid.

Hiring was part of it too. Applift helped identify the right people for RiseUp's mobile team, brought them in during the engagement, and made sure actual knowledge transferred, not just a repo. Self-sufficiency was the point.

## The Outcome

Two apps shipped. Both native, iOS and Android, built on the infrastructure and standards set during the engagement. That foundation became what RiseUp's own team inherited.

The handoff was clean. Their mobile engineers came up through the project, hired with Applift's help and brought in during the work. By the end, they owned the codebase. The engagement closed the way it was meant to: with RiseUp not needing Applift anymore.

Meanwhile, their customers had noticed. Average cash flow improvement hit $340 a month within three months of using the app.
