## Bridging the Mobile Gap
RiseUp had established a trusted digital platform for financial wellness, but the lack of a native mobile presence created visible friction in the user experience. They didn’t just need developers; they required a partner to own the technical foundation and make critical architectural calls early.

## Engineering and Talent Integration
Applift deployed a squad of senior developers to establish the infrastructure and development standards that would carry RiseUp's mobile growth. Parallel to the technical build, we led a strategic talent acquisition effort to identify and onboard RiseUp’s internal mobile team. By embedding these new hires directly into the project during development, we facilitated a deep transfer of institutional knowledge rather than just a code handoff.
