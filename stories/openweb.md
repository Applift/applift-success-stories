# OpenWeb

OpenWeb's mission is to improve online conversation. As a product company, OpenWeb partners with publishers and brands to build strong, direct relationships with their audiences. OpenWeb's technology empowers its partners to build vibrant communities rooted in healthy conversations and robust social experiences. OpenWeb works with more than 1,000 top-tier publishers, hosting more than 100 million active users each month.

Our team is developing the iOS, Android, and React Native SDKs for the #1 conversation tool in the world. The SDKs consist of a variety of customizable UIs and provide high levels of flexibility during the integration process. Monetization is supported through the integration with the Google Ads SDK.

## Engineering Focus

- Multi-Platform SDK Development: Developing native iOS, Android, and React Native SDKs in parallel, enabling publishers to embed OpenWeb's conversation platform on any mobile stack.
- Customizable UI System: Building a rich set of customizable UI components that allow publishers to tailor the conversation experience to match their brand identity and design language.
- Monetization Integration: Integrating the Google Ads SDK to enable publishers to monetize their conversation spaces, creating a sustainable revenue stream alongside user engagement.
- Flexible Integration Architecture: Designing the SDKs with high levels of integration flexibility, ensuring smooth adoption across diverse publisher tech stacks and configurations.

## Technical Stack

- **Platform**: iOS, Android, React Native
- **Monetization**: Google Ads SDK
- **Scale**: 1,000+ publishers, 100M+ monthly active users

## The Impact

Our SDKs power the conversation experience for more than 1,000 top-tier publishers and 100 million active users each month. By delivering cross-platform SDKs with customizable UIs and built-in monetization, we help OpenWeb fulfill its mission of improving online conversation at a global scale.
