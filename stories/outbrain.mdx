<Challenge glanceItems={[

Outbrain is a leading recommendation platform for the open web, enabling one-third of the world's online consumers to discover content through recommendation feeds. The Smartfeed Mobile SDK needed to fetch and render content recommendations inside top-tier publisher applications with low latency, high reliability, and a flexible integration layer that works across diverse publisher tech stacks.
</Challenge>

<Partnership>
Building an SDK that runs inside top-tier publisher apps like CNN and ESPN requires a different kind of accountability — every performance regression or crash is felt by millions of users. Applift's engineers worked within Outbrain's mobile team with that level of ownership, treating the SDK as if it were their own product.

The collaboration was hands-on and iterative: working closely with Outbrain's product and backend teams to optimize recommendation fetching, minimize latency, and ensure the SDK integrated smoothly across diverse publisher tech stacks without disrupting existing app experiences.
</Partnership>
