## About

Battery Ventures is a global, technology-focused investment firm backing companies across software, industrial technology, consumer, and fintech - from seed stage through growth equity.

## The challenge

Battery’s investment team tackled the same core questions every day: Which companies are raising? Who’s behind them? What does their technical footprint look like?

The answers existed across Crunchbase, Salesforce, GitHub, and internal research notes - fragmented, inconsistent, and time-consuming to piece together.

They needed a single interface that could unify the data, normalize it, and remain seamless across browsers, tabs, and time zones.

## The Outcome

As an embedded engineering partner, we owned the full stack - frontend, backend, data pipelines, and infrastructure - and worked closely with multiple teams across Battery to support a wide range of workflows and use cases.

Together, we developed features tailored to different parts of the organization, helping turn repetitive, manual processes into streamlined, automated systems wherever possible.

The platform became a shared layer across the organization - connecting data, workflows, and internal processes into a faster and more scalable operating model.
