## About

Battery Ventures is a global, technology-focused investment firm backing companies across software, industrial technology, consumer, and fintech — from seed stage through growth equity.

## The challenge

Battery's investment team works through the same questions daily: which companies are raising, who's behind them, what does the technical footprint look like. The answers were scattered across Crunchbase, Salesforce, GitHub, and internal notes.

They needed one interface to pull it all together, normalize it, and stay useful across browsers, tabs, and time zones.

## What we built

The **web apps** handle deal research, portfolio analysis, and complex queries across sources. Built for people who live in investment data all day — density and speed over visual minimalism.

The **Chrome extension** brings it into the browser. An analyst on a company page or GitHub profile pulls up relevant data without switching tabs.

Underneath sits a **backend** that ingests each source through its own pipeline into a shared data model. New sources plug in without rearchitecting.

On top, a **query engine** handles the way investment professionals actually search — something like *"Series B healthcare companies that raised in the last 18 months, where we have a relationship, and engineering headcount grew 30%+."* Indexed, pre-joined datasets with faceted filters and boolean logic; the user just types and gets results.
