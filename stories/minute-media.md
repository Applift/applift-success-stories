# Minute Media

Minute Media is a leading technology and digital content company. Their proprietary video and multimedia publishing platform, Voltax, powers the creation, distribution, consumption and monetization of third party publishers and advertisers.

We are developing the Voltax Video Mobile SDK. Voltax Video is a cutting-edge, Online Video Platform for publishers that's customizable and offers robust content and monetization options.

## Engineering Focus

- Mobile SDK Development: Building the Voltax Video Mobile SDK, enabling publishers to integrate a full-featured video platform into their mobile applications.
- Video Playback & Performance: Engineering high-performance video rendering with smooth playback, adaptive streaming, and minimal latency across devices.
- Monetization Integration: Implementing robust ad-serving capabilities within the SDK, supporting diverse monetization strategies for publishers and advertisers.
- Publisher Customization: Designing a flexible SDK architecture that allows publishers to tailor the video experience to their brand and content needs.

## Technical Stack

- **Platform**: iOS, Android
- **Domain**: Mobile Video SDK
- **Capabilities**: Video Playback, Ad Monetization, Publisher Integration

## The Impact

Our work on the Voltax Video Mobile SDK gives Minute Media's publisher network a powerful, customizable video solution for mobile. By delivering a performant SDK with built-in monetization, we help publishers and advertisers maximize the value of their video content across millions of mobile users.
