## About
Zencity is a civic technology company whose platform equips local governments with community insights and decision-making tools. Having established a mature desktop product, Zencity leveraged Applift’s mobile expertise to bring that experience to iOS.

## The Partnership
We embedded directly with Zencity's product and design teams to define the mobile app's scope and technical direction. Beyond execution, Applift drove the architectural decisions required to align the mobile app with the existing product ecosystem. A key technical focus was the seamless integration of web-native components; we developed customized WebView implementations that allowed specific web UI elements to function as first-class citizens within the native app, maintaining brand consistency while optimizing for mobile performance.

Zencity launched a robust native iOS application that extends their platform's reach without compromising the depth of their web product. Through Applift’s ownership of the end-to-end execution - from complex UI bridging to final deployment, Zencity successfully delivered a high-quality mobile experience that meets the rigorous standards of government technology.
