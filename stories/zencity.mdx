<ExecutiveSummary>
Partnering with Zencity's product and design leadership, we took full ownership of extending their established desktop platform into a native iOS application - architecting a hybrid solution that preserves the richness of their web experience within a performant mobile shell.
</ExecutiveSummary>

## About
Zencity is a civic technology company whose platform equips local governments with community insights and decision-making tools. Having established a mature desktop product, Zencity sought to bring that experience to mobile.

## The Partnership
We embedded directly with Zencity's PM and design team to define the mobile app's scope, shape its design direction, and align it with the existing product ecosystem. From there, we assumed end-to-end ownership of the build - translating strategic intent into a shipped native iOS application.

## The Outcome
Zencity launched a native iOS application that extends their platform into mobile without compromising the depth of their web product - delivered through a collaborative partnership in which we owned execution from scope to ship.
