Zencity reinvents the way local governments make decisions. Their platform helps cities understand their residents' real needs and priorities. They needed a trusted partner to take full ownership of their iOS application — from feature planning through App Store delivery — so their core team could focus on the platform. The app required push notifications, deep linking, and secure Auth0 authentication for government stakeholders.
</Challenge>

<Partnership>
Zencity needed more than a contractor — they needed someone who could own the iOS app entirely so their core team could focus on the platform. Applift stepped into that role with full autonomy: managing the development lifecycle end-to-end, from feature planning and implementation through App Store submission and release management.

The trust required to hand over a government-facing product is significant. Applift earned it through consistent delivery, proactive communication, and a deep understanding of the compliance and security standards required when serving public sector stakeholders.
</Partnership>
