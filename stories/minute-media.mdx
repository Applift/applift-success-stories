## About

Minute Media is a leading technology and digital content company. Their proprietary video and multimedia publishing platform, Voltax, powers the creation, distribution, consumption, and monetization of content for third-party publishers and advertisers.

## Native SDK Engineering
We spearheaded the technical architecture and end-to-end development of the Voltax Video Mobile SDK for iOS and Android.
* **Architecture & Development:** Engineered the core SDK logic in Swift and Kotlin, ensuring a lightweight footprint with maximum performance for video rendering.
* **DevOps & Distribution:** Built and managed the entire CI/CD pipeline to automate testing and deployment. We handled the process of publishing to **CocoaPods** (iOS) and **Maven Central** (Android), ensuring seamless integration for third-party developers.
* **Technical Documentation:** Wrote comprehensive, developer-centric documentation to ensure clear onboarding and implementation for Minute Media’s partners.

## Strategic Web Development & UI Excellence
Working as an extension of Minute Media’s internal frontend team, we helped bring complex publisher visions to life, including high-profile sites like **The Players' Tribune**. 

* **Collaborative Workflow:** We embedded directly with their PMs, designers, and internal engineers to ensure every feature aligned with the platform's long-term roadmap.
* **Performance & Responsiveness:** Beyond standard implementation, we focused on "mobile-first" responsiveness and fluid animations that maintain brand integrity across all device types and browsers.
* **Custom UI Components:** Developed specialized React components designed for high engagement, prioritizing both SEO performance and a premium user experience.
