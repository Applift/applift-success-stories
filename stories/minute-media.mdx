Minute Media is a leading technology and digital content company. Their proprietary platform, Voltax, powers the creation, distribution, consumption, and monetization of content for third-party publishers and advertisers. They needed a mobile video SDK that delivers high-performance video playback with adaptive streaming, built-in ad monetization, and enough flexibility for publishers to tailor the experience to their brand.
</Challenge>

<Partnership>
Video SDKs are uniquely demanding — performance issues are immediately visible to users, and publisher expectations for playback quality are sky-high. Applift's team worked within Minute Media's engineering group to build the Voltax Mobile SDK with the same care and ownership as an in-house team.

The collaboration focused on getting the details right: adaptive streaming across different network conditions, smooth ad transitions that don't break the viewing experience, and an architecture flexible enough for publishers to customize without compromising performance.
</Partnership>
