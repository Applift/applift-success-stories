# Mako

Mako is the Keshet Media Group's digital arm. With an average of 20M views per month, Mako is an independent news portal and one of Israel's top three most visited websites.

We are working with the Mako frontend team to build 12+ smart TV and web apps using React.js and Next.js. 12+ is Israel's No1 streaming platform. 12+ is the new and improved way to watch channel 12 live and VOD content, including Channel 12 news and Israel's leading reality shows, comedy and drama, such as Wedding at First Sight, The Next Star, Ninja Israel, the beauty and the baker, Master Chef, Ofira and Berkowitz, and more.

## Engineering Focus

- Smart TV Application Development: Building and maintaining 12+ applications across a wide range of smart TV platforms, delivering a consistent streaming experience on every device.
- Web Application Development: Developing high-performance web applications using React.js and Next.js to serve millions of monthly viewers with fast load times and smooth navigation.
- Live & VOD Streaming: Engineering reliable live streaming and video-on-demand capabilities to support Channel 12's full content library, from news to prime-time entertainment.
- Cross-Platform Consistency: Ensuring a unified user experience across smart TVs, web browsers, and diverse device form factors at scale.

## Technical Stack

- **Frontend**: React.js, Next.js
- **Platforms**: Smart TVs, Web
- **Content**: Live Streaming, VOD

## The Impact

Our collaboration with Mako powers 12+, Israel's leading streaming platform, across 12+ smart TV and web applications. By delivering a scalable, high-performance frontend architecture, we help Mako serve 20M+ monthly views with a seamless viewing experience—from live news to the country's most-watched entertainment.
