# Mako

Building 12+ smart TV and web apps for **Keshet Media Group's** digital arm, serving an average of **20M views per month**.

## The Challenge

Mako, the digital platform of Keshet Media Group, needed a reliable development partner to build and maintain a growing portfolio of smart TV and web applications across multiple platforms and devices.

## Our Approach

- Developed **12+ applications** spanning smart TVs and web platforms
- Built with **React.js**, **Next.js**, and **TypeScript** for maximum code reuse
- Implemented performance-first architecture to handle massive concurrent viewership
- Established CI/CD pipelines for rapid, reliable deployments across all platforms

## Tech Stack

- React.js
- Next.js
- TypeScript

## Results

- **20M+ monthly views** across all platforms
- **12+ apps** delivered and maintained
- Seamless cross-platform user experience
