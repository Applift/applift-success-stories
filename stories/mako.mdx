Applift engineers worked inside Mako's frontend team, building and maintaining the 12+ apps across smart TVs and web, from video player internals to store submissions.
</ExecutiveSummary>

12+ is Israel's biggest streaming platform. Channel 12 live broadcasts, news, reality, comedy, drama on demand. Mako, Keshet Media Group's digital side, pulls in over 20M views a month across web and connected TV.

Applift's engineers worked inside Mako's frontend group. They were in the standups, in the code reviews, making architecture calls alongside everyone else.

<Partnership>
This was one of Applift's longest running partnerships. The same engineers who built the first smart TV apps kept shipping features for years. They knew the codebases, the platform quirks, and the team's way of working. A new device or OS version didn't require weeks of onboarding.

When a live broadcast needed to work or a new TV platform needed support, the engineers already had context. They picked it up and shipped it.
</Partnership>
