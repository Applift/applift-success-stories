Mako is the Keshet Media Group's digital arm and one of Israel's top three most visited websites. Their streaming platform, 12+, needed to deliver live and VOD content reliably across a wide range of smart TV platforms and web browsers — each with different capabilities and constraints. The challenge was building and maintaining 12+ applications with a consistent, high-performance streaming experience across all devices.
</Challenge>

<Solution clientInfo={{ description: "Mako is Keshet Media Group's digital arm and one of Israel's top three most visited websites, with 20M+ views per month." }}>

**Smart TV Applications**

Building and maintaining applications across a wide range of smart TV platforms with a consistent streaming experience.

**Web Applications**

High-performance web apps using React.js and Next.js to serve millions of monthly viewers.

**Live & VOD Streaming**

Reliable live streaming and video-on-demand for Channel 12's full content library, from news to prime-time entertainment.

**Cross-Platform Consistency**

Unified user experience across smart TVs, web browsers, and diverse device form factors at scale.
</Solution>
