## About

Unit is the banking-as-a-service platform that lets companies embed financial features directly into their products. Its white-label UIs provide pre-built, customizable components that let Unit's clients build in minutes and launch in weeks.

## SDK Engineering

We are the team behind Unit's client-facing SDKs, working in lockstep with Unit's internal web team. From day one, iOS (Swift), Android (Kotlin), and React Native have been developed in parallel, with full ownership of architecture, development, and ongoing evolution. The SDKs ship inside the production apps of Unit's clients.
