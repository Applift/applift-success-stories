Unit is the banking-as-a-service platform that lets companies embed powerful financial features into their product. They needed multiple native mobile SDKs for their clients, comprehensive implementation guides, and hands-on integration support. A key challenge was building a Mobile SDK for adding Unit credit cards to Apple Wallet, Google Pay, and Samsung Pay using Visa's push provisioning — a highly regulated, technically complex integration.
</Challenge>

<Partnership>
Working in a highly regulated financial environment demands precision and deep technical trust. Applift's engineers embedded into Unit's mobile workflow, collaborating closely with their backend and compliance teams to navigate Visa's push provisioning requirements — a process with zero margin for error.

The team brought hands-on integration support to Unit's own clients, helping them adopt the SDKs successfully. This wasn't just about building tools; it was about ensuring every downstream integration worked flawlessly in a space where reliability and compliance are non-negotiable.
</Partnership>
